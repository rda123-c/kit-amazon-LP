Kit N/A contains less than 0.5% ABV, which is the legal and industry-standard definition of "non-alcoholic." For context, many fruit juices and kombucha naturally contain similar trace amounts. Kit N/A is safe for people avoiding alcohol and won't cause intoxication.
Kit N/A is gluten-removed — it's brewed with malted barley and then processed to remove gluten to below 20 ppm (the FDA threshold for gluten-free). It's not certified gluten-free, but most people with gluten sensitivities enjoy it without issue. If you have celiac disease, please consult your doctor.
Flavor in beer comes from four places: water, malt, hops, and yeast. Kit N/A uses all four — premium hop varieties like Simcoe, Idaho 7, El Dorado, and Amarillo — to create complex, layered flavor profiles that are just as satisfying as any craft beer you've had.
